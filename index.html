What We Do



At NanyaCredit, our goal is to work with employers to relieve the burden of requests from their employees for financial assistance. Bank loans take weeks to secure, come at high-interestrates and require paperwork from both the employee and the employer. Our goal is to make the entire credit process as effortlessand hassle-free as possible, so you can focus on what’s really important - your business.




How It Works



Apply Online

Employee applies for a loan on his mobile, tablest or PC

Yay! Get the credit

Real time verification. Once verified, money is sent to the employee's bank account

Repayment

Done by the employer in the form of monthly deduction from the employee's salary
